Referral Program

Community Partners can help people start without giving legal advice.

A Community Partner is a person, business, organization, event, or community location that helps someone start Smarter Justice and receive a private continuation link. Community Partners do not give legal advice, promise outcomes, or complete the application for the user.

How credit works

Each Community Partner receives a unique code, private dashboard link, and flyer link. People start through the tracked code, and the private dashboard shows referral totals and credit status without exposing user names, emails, documents, or legal topics.

Generated flyers avoid showing a plain website address and use a tracked QR code/link.
