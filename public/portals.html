Why separate portals?

Clearer for users

A person with an IRS notice needs tax-specific language. A person planning a will needs estate-planning language. The umbrella helps them choose.

Safer for review

Different areas need different questions, forms, source checks, and professional-review rules.

Easier to grow

Each portal can grow independently while using shared private links, document uploads, review options, payments, and Community Partner tools when appropriate.

Smarter Justice is a private support service, not a law firm and not the government. Focused portals may have separate services, review steps, pricing, and terms. No guaranteed outcomes.
