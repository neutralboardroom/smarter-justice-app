Your information

Privacy

Smarter Justice is designed to collect only the information needed to provide the feature or service you choose.

Information you provide

This may include account details, professional-profile information, starting questions, documents you choose to upload, referral information, payment references, and communications with Smarter Justice.

How information may be used

Information may be used to save your work, provide requested tools, organize a matter, review a profile claim, manage membership, communicate with you, protect the platform, and improve services. Smarter Justice does not sell personal information to advertisers.

Professional profiles

Some professional profiles begin with lawfully available public information. Public sources and profile status are shown where available. An unclaimed profile does not mean the professional participates in or endorses Smarter Justice. Professionals may request to claim, correct, or otherwise address profile information.

Sharing with professionals

A user’s private matter information or documents are not shared with a professional merely because the professional is listed or pays for membership. Sharing requires a specific user-authorized feature and appropriate safeguards.

Payments

When payment is available, payment processing is handled through the displayed payment provider. Smarter Justice may retain transaction references and membership status. Hosted payment checkout keeps complete card details with the payment provider rather than Smarter Justice.

Security and retention

Access controls, private links, secure sessions, logging, and storage protections are used or required according to the feature involved. Information is retained only as long as reasonably needed for service, legal, security, and recordkeeping purposes. Before submitting especially sensitive information, confirm that the page expressly states the secure feature is active.

Your choices

You may choose not to provide optional information. You may contact Smarter Justice to ask about account information, profile corrections, or deletion where applicable, subject to legal and recordkeeping requirements.

Contact

Use the contact page for privacy questions or requests.

Effective July 20, 2026. Additional portal-specific privacy notices may apply when a focused portal handles specialized information.
