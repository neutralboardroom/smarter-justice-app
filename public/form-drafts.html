Selected Form Support

Form drafts are prepared carefully, one source-checked path at a time.

For selected paths, users can add missing details and receive a worksheet or draft for review. These are not filed forms and are not sign-ready until source checks, missing-answer review, Human Review Specialist review, and user verification are complete.

Current safeguards

  • Users can add missing details from the dashboard.
  • Reviewers can correct draft details without changing the user’s original answers.
  • Missing information and supporting documents must be addressed before a draft is marked ready.
  • Approval status, missing required fields, uploaded documents, and reviewer notes are considered together.

Safety rules

  • Human Review Specialist approval is required before a review-ready draft is delivered.
  • Official source, instructions, filing method, fees, signature requirements, and version/date must be checked.
  • Missing required fields block delivery.
  • Attorney, tax attorney, CPA, enrolled agent, accountant, benefits professional, or injury attorney review may be recommended.
  • Smarter Justice does not guarantee approval, acceptance, settlement, benefits, timing, refund, or legal/tax outcome.
