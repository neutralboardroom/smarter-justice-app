Protecting access

Security

Smarter Justice separates public information from private accounts, documents, professional controls, and private management tools.

Private access

Saved user work, professional accounts, partner reporting, authorized team tools, and protected management controls use separate protected access methods.

Limited information

Public pages and profile searches display only information approved for public viewing. Private documents and matter details are not intended for public search results.

Safer files

Sensitive upload services use appropriate limits, file-type checks, private storage, scanning, retention controls, and authorized access controls before they are offered publicly.

Before sending sensitive information

Use only the secure upload or account feature provided for that purpose. Do not send passwords, full payment-card details, or highly sensitive information through ordinary contact messages.

Professional and team responsibilities

Professionals and authorized team members must protect account access, use information only for approved purposes, follow confidentiality requirements, and report suspected unauthorized access promptly.

Ongoing improvement

Security requirements increase as services move from testing to controlled pilots and broader public use. A sensitive feature is not presented as active until the required protections have been configured and tested.

Report a concern

Use the contact page to report a security concern. Do not include unnecessary confidential details in the initial message.
