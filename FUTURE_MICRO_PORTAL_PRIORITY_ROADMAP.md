## Active planned legal micro-portals

### DigitalDivorce

Include divorce, separation, annulment, contested/uncontested routing, forms, financial disclosures, parenting plans, child and spousal support, property and debt division, settlement and separation agreements, service and filing preparation, safety screening, mediation, attorney review, and post-judgment modification or enforcement.

### Motor Vehicle / Personal Injury Help Center

Include accidents, evidence preservation, insurance, police reports, property damage, no-fault/PIP, UM/UIM, medical records and bills, wage loss, treatment timelines, demand-support organization, consultation, claim lifecycle, and attorney engagement. Medical malpractice and workers’ compensation remain separate high-risk specialties even when cross-routed.

### Criminal Law Help Center

Include arrest and charge organization, court-date and release-condition tracking, document explanation, evidence and witness organization, collateral-consequence screening, attorney consultation, and jurisdiction-specific routing. Do not imply defense representation or provide tactics that require licensed professional judgment.

### Medical Malpractice Assistant Center

Include chronology, provider and facility mapping, record and bill organization, consent forms, expert-review readiness, statute-of-limitations urgency, damages organization, and attorney consultation. This is a separate high-risk portal and should not be buried inside ordinary personal injury.

### Family Law Help Center

Include custody, visitation, support, paternity, domestic-violence safety routing, guardianship, adoption, relocation, enforcement, and modification. Coordinate with DigitalDivorce while preserving different stages and safety needs.

### Social Security and Disability Help Center

Include SSDI, SSI, initial applications, reconsideration, hearings, medical evidence, work history, denials, deadlines, representative routing, and benefit lifecycle. Veterans and other public benefits may begin as coordinated lanes but may later warrant separate portals.

### Bankruptcy and Debt Help Center

Include bankruptcy organizers, means-test and schedule readiness, debt lawsuits, collections, judgments, garnishment, settlement organization, credit counseling, notices, deadlines, and attorney routing.

### Workers’ Compensation Help Center

Include workplace injury reporting, employer/carrier notices, medical and wage records, hearings, benefit status, retaliation issue spotting, deadlines, and attorney consultation.

### Employment and Labor Law Help Center

Include wages, overtime, discrimination, harassment, retaliation, leave, accommodations, unemployment, contracts and severance, workplace records, union/labor matters where appropriate, agency charges, deadlines, and attorney review.

### Tenant / Landlord Help Center

Include eviction, rent, repairs, habitability, deposits, leases, notices, housing-court documents, landlord operations, foreclosure-adjacent routing, local rules, deadlines, and attorney or mediation support. User-side selection is essential.
