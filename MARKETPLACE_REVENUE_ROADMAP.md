# Smarter Justice Marketplace Revenue Roadmap

Version 1.1.0

## Active founding-pilot revenue foundation

- $15 monthly or $150 annual individual founding-professional membership.
- Firm membership billed per covered attorney or professional seat.
- Volume discounts: 10% for 2–4 seats, 15% for 5–9, 20% for 10–24, and 25% for 25 or more.
- Central firm billing, firm administrators, roster controls, and individual profiles.
- Stripe recurring subscription checkout and signed-webhook confirmation foundation.
- In-person building and firm outreach, QR/mobile enrollment, campaign attribution, follow-up, potential-seat tracking, and projected recurring revenue.

The introductory rate is an acquisition experiment, not a permanent promise. Membership does not guarantee users, appointments, ranking, eligibility, revenue, exclusivity, or outcomes.

## Preserved revenue programs

- Paid individual professional memberships.
- Firm and multi-office memberships.
- Enhanced profile subscriptions.
- Clearly labeled sponsored placements.
- Scheduling and professional-dashboard tools.
- Document, form, contract, will, tax, and other review workflow tools.
- Analytics and conversion reporting.
- Staff accounts and additional seats.
- Practice-growth, follow-up, content, and verified review-request tools.
- Fixed technology or platform charges where legally approved.
- CRM, calendar, intake, and practice-management integrations.
- White-label, enterprise, institutional, API, implementation, migration, and training services.

## Additional models to evaluate

- premium AI-assisted intake and organized matter-preparation tools;
- embeddable contact, questionnaire, profile, and booking widgets;
- fixed-price service catalogs and verified-service packages;
- multi-location and practice-group routing;
- secure document workspace, e-signature, communication, and lifecycle add-ons;
- non-sensitive benchmarking and attribution analytics;
- co-branded community, employer, association, union, nonprofit, and institutional access programs;
- data-room, deadline, renewal, compliance, and obligation-management subscriptions;
- professional content, Q&A, webinars, and education tools;
- managed intake and follow-up services for firms.

## Required controls

Every program records status, audience, pricing, billing model, compliance gate, owner decision, and portal applicability. Payment may not affect AI risk analysis, warnings, credentials, verification, ratings, neutral eligibility, or claims that a professional is the best provider. Outcome-based and percentage professional-fee models remain blocked absent exact jurisdiction-specific approval.

## Public experience rule

Customer and professional-facing pages must use plain language, clearly separate available features from planned capabilities, and be reviewed on phone, tablet, and desktop before release. Internal eligibility logic, configuration, technical evidence, and staff operations remain on protected surfaces.
