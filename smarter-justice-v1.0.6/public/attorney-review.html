Review Options

Internal human review is separate from attorney, tax, and professional review.

Smarter Justice can organize a file internally first, then recommend the right professional review when needed.

Human Review Specialist

Internal review to organize facts, documents, uploads, deadlines, checklists, and starter worksheets. This is not legal advice, tax advice, or professional representation.

Attorney review

Recommended for higher-risk legal issues, court matters, state-law disputes, immigration complexity, bankruptcy, injury, malpractice, family law, criminal/record cleanup, IP disputes, appeals, and other legal questions.

Tax professional review

Taxes may recommend a CPA, enrolled agent, accountant, tax attorney, or other qualified professional. Offer in compromise, liens, levies, audits, collections, payroll tax, tax court, and major tax debt are review-heavy.

Other qualified review

Benefits advocates, housing counselors, education advocates, mediators, insurance professionals, and other qualified reviewers may be recommended where appropriate.
