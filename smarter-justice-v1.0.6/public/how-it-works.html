How It Works

Start free, organize the problem, then choose the right level of review.

Smarter Justice is built for people who do not know where to begin. It separates internal human organization from attorney, tax, benefits, or other professional review.

1

Ask a free starting question

Describe the problem in plain language. Upload a notice, PDF, screenshot, email, photo, letter, court paper, tax notice, benefits notice, accident letter, or business filing if you have one.

2

Choose state and local details when needed

Divorce, family, housing, probate, real estate, record cleanup, traffic, workers’ compensation, injury, malpractice, and many other matters depend on state, county, city, court, or agency.

3

Get an organized starting file

Smarter Justice saves documents, identifies possible concerns that may need review, and gives a plain-language checklist of likely next steps.

4

Human review is separate

An internal Human Review Specialist can organize the file, ask follow-up questions, and prepare starter worksheets or file summaries. This is separate from attorney or professional review.

5

Professional review when needed

Depending on the issue, attorney, tax attorney, CPA, enrolled-agent, accountant, benefits professional, advocate, mediator, or other qualified review may be recommended.

6

Completed forms only after review

Where a path is verified and safe, completed forms may be delivered after review. For complex or unverified paths, Smarter Justice prepares worksheets, summaries, document checklists, and organized review files.
