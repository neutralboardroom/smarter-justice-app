Forms Roadmap

Completed forms should happen only when the source, field mapping, and review path are ready.

Smarter Justice is built to move toward AI-filled, review-ready forms, but v1.0.6 uses safety levels and a first verified-form-path registry so the platform does not overpromise automation before a form path is verified.

LevelUser meaningSmarter Justice action
0Source catalog onlyTrack the official source and route to review before specific form guidance.
1Worksheet onlyCollect facts, save documents, and create an organized starter file for human review.
2Field-mapped draft foundationBuild verified answer-to-field mapping before producing completed drafts.
3Review-ready completed draftGenerate completed drafts only after Human Review Specialist review.
4Sign-ready after reviewDeliver completed forms only after review, user verification, and payment where applicable.
5Filing support where allowedFuture filing support only where permitted and technically/compliance ready.

Official government forms are often free from the government. Smarter Justice charges only for support, organization, review, and preparation services where available.

First verified-form-path foundation

v1.0.6 adds a reusable registry for selected paths such as IRS offer in compromise starter files, IRS installment agreement starter files, individual tax preparation organizers, bankruptcy debt schedules, Social Security disability appeal organizers, business/EIN formation organizers, trademark starting files, state divorce/family starting files, vehicle accident document organization, and medical malpractice records organization.

These paths are designed to gather missing information, create organized files, generate a form-draft starter package, and guide Human Review Specialist work. They are not a promise that every official form is completed automatically today.
